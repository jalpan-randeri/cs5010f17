Big-O Notation is Mathematical

O(f), Ω(f), Θ(f)

Some of you may think big-O notation has to do with the efficiency of algorithms. That's a common misconception.

Big-O notation is often used to talk about the efficiency of algorithms, but the notation itself is purely mathematical. Big-O notation is about sets, functions, and real numbers.

Big-O notation is not about algorithms, programming, or computer science. The reason we use big-O notation so much in courses on algorithms, programming, and computer science is simple: those courses discuss the asymptotic growth of mathematical functions.

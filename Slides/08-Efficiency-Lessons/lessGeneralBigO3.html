Less General Definitions of O(f), Ω(f), Θ(f)

A less general definition of Ω(f) can be obtained by changing  ≤  to  ≥  in the less general definition of O(f). A less general definition of Θ(f) can then be obtained by taking the intersection of those less general definitions of O(f) and Ω(f).

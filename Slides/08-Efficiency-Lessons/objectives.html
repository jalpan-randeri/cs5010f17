Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you should be able to:

  • define O(f), Ω(f), and Θ(f)
  • tell whether A is a subset of B whenever A and B are defined as a complexity class of the form O(f), Ω(f), or Θ(f) where f is a polynomial, exponential, logarithmic, or log-linear function defined on non-negative real numbers
